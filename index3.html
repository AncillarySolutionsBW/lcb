Our Story

Where friendship
meets purpose.

Ladies Circle Botswana was formed in 1980 and subscribes to the aims, objectives, and motto of Ladies Circle International — an organisation founded in England in 1930, now spanning 36 countries with over 13,000 women worldwide.

We are 32 women across four circles — three in Gaborone and one in Francistown — raising funds, hosting service events, and supporting community organisations across the country.

"Friendship & Service"

The motto of Ladies Circle International since 1930

Ladies Circle became fully independent from the Round Table association at the 1994 AGM. Membership is open to all women aged 18 to 45. Projects are financed through private donations, themed events, tea parties, merchandise, and raffles.

Membership

Become part of
the circle.

Ladies Circle Botswana is open to all women aged 18 to 45. If you believe in the power of friendship and service, we would love to have you join one of our four circles in Gaborone or Francistown.

Members participate in regular fellowship meetings, lead and support service projects, and become part of a global community of women committed to making a difference.
