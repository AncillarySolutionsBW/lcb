Our Story

Friendship
in action.

Ladies Circle Botswana was formed in 1980 and subscribes to the aims, objectives, and motto of Ladies Circle International — an association born in England in 1930 and now spanning 36 countries with over 13,000 women worldwide.

Today we bring together 32 women across four active circles — three in Gaborone and one in Francistown. Together we raise funds, host community events, and deliver practical support to organisations across Botswana.

Ladies Circle is open to all women aged 18 to 45. We believe that service is most powerful when built on genuine friendship.
